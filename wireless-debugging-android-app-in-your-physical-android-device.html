Wireless debugging android app in your physical android device

Anindra September 22, 2022

There are two options to pair it with your computer, using a QR code or pairing a device using a six-digit code.

Now come to your PC or laptop, for windows devices double click on adb.exe and type this$ adb pair 192.168.0.267:34568 #this will be defferent in your case#and after this enter your Wi-Fi pairing codeEnter pairing code: 765467Successfully paired to 192.168.0.267:34568

For macOS or Linux open the terminal and type this$ adb connect 192.168.0.267:34568 #this will be defferent in your case#and after this enter your Wi-Fi pairing codeEnter pairing code: 765467Successfully paired to 192.168.0.267:34568

Rooted devices

For rooted devices simply download wireless debugging app example Wireless ADB: ADB over TCP/IP, WADB - Simple wireless ADB enabler, ADB Wifi, etc.

After downloading complete open app and enabling wireless, and app will show IP(example 192.168.0.267) this will help to connect via and.

Now open adb.exe in windows device and type this commandadb connect 192.168.0.267

For macOS and Linuxadb connect 192.168.0.267
